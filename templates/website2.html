About Us

KMCT College of Engineering, Kozhikode, established in 2001, is affiliated with KTU and accredited by AICTE.

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in technology and engineering disciplines, fostering excellence and innovation.

Our commitment is to provide students with a high-quality education and prepare them for success in the global technology industry.
